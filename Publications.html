I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Chennai Mathematical Insitute, Chennai, India. Previously, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Toronto, Toronto, Canada.

I did my PhD at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai, India, under the supervision of Prof. S. Gun. The subject of my thesis was the study of Euclidean ideal classes appearing in the class groups of rings of integers. My research interests range over topics in Algebraic Number theory and Sieve methods.
