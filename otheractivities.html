Other activities

I am an Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Thiruvananthapuram. Prior to this, I spent ten months as an INSPIRE Faculty fellow at the Indian Statistical Insitute, Bangalore, two years as a postdoctoral fellow at Chennai Mathematical Institute (under the supervision of Prof. P. Rath) and a year and a half as a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Toronto, Toronto, Canada (under the supervision of Prof. V. Kumar Murty).

I did my PhD at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai, India, under the supervision of Prof. S. Gun. The subject of my thesis was the study of Euclidean ideal classes appearing in the class groups of rings of integers. My research interests range over topics in Algebraic Number theory and Sieve methods.
